About Us

Dr. Ashish Chopra(PT) with 12+ Years of experience and expertise in Orthopaedics, Neurology, Cardiology & Paediatrics Physiotherapy years of practice with Apollo Clinic now open as Dr. Ashish Chopra’s (PT) Physiotherapy & Posture Clinic at Gujranwala Town.

Clinic is a specialized healthcare facility where physiotherapists provide assessment, diagnosis, and treatment for patients with various physical conditions and movement disorders. We are specifically designed to cater to the needs of individuals seeking physiotherapy services.

What We Do

Here are some key aspects of Dr. Ashish Chopra Physiotherapy Clinic:

Musculoskeletal Injuries

Help with injuries to the muscles, bones, joints, ligaments, and tendons, such as sprains, strains, fractures, and post-operative rehabilitation.

Neurological disorders

Assist individuals with conditions affecting the nervous system, such as stroke, spinal cord injuries, multiple sclerosis and Parkinson's disease.

Cardiorespiratory conditions

Treatment for individuals with heart and lung-related problems, including COPD, asthma, and cardiac rehabilitation.

Sports injuries

Treating sports-related injuries, helping athletes recover from sprains, strains, fractures, and other athletic injuries.

Pediatric conditions

Work with children to improve their motor skills, treat developmental delays, and manage conditions like cerebral palsy.

Take Appointment For Better Treatment

Reach Out and Inquire: Call the clinic during their operating hours to inquire about availability and scheduling an appointment.

Provide Necessary Information: The clinic may ask for your personal details such as name, contact information, and possibly some medical history. This helps them understand your needs and assign the appropriate healthcare professional for your appointment.

Specify Preferred Date and Time: Let the clinic know your preferred date and time for the appointment. Depending on their availability, they will offer you a suitable time slot for Clinic Appointment or Home Visit depends on the Medical Condition.
